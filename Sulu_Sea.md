# [[Sulu_Sea]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Sulu_Sea,160194|WD~Sulu_Sea,160194]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Sulu Sea (Filipino: Dagat Sulu; Tausug: Dagat sin S큰g; Malay: Laut Sulu; Spanish: Mar de Jol처) 
> is a body of water in the southwestern area of the [[../Philippines|Philippines]] , 
> separated from the South China Sea in the northwest by Palawan 
> and from the Celebes Sea in the southeast by the Sulu Archipelago. 
> Borneo is found to the southwest and Visayas to the northeast.
>
> The Sulu Sea contains a number of islands. 
> The Cuyo Islands and the Cagayan Islands are part of the province of Palawan 
> whereas Mapun and the Turtle Islands are part of the province of Tawi-Tawi. 
> 
> Sulu Sea is also where the Tubbataha Reef National Marine Park, 
> one of the World Heritage Sites is located.
>
> Panay Gulf is an extension of the Sulu Sea.  
> Straits out of the Sulu Sea include the Iloilo Strait, the Guimaras Strait, and the Basilan Strait.
>
